My Daily Routine When The School Is Open

When school is open, my days follow a clear routine. The alarm usually rings at 6:30 AM, pulling me out of bed. First, I get ready, which means brushing my teeth, washing my face, and putting on my school uniform. Then, I head to the kitchen for a quick breakfast. My mom usually has something ready, like toast or cereal, and I drink a glass of milk. After breakfast, I grab my backpack and head out the door. The walk to school takes about fifteen minutes, and I often meet friends along the way. School starts at 8:00 AM, and the day is filled with various subjects, from math to history. Lunch break is a welcome pause to eat and chat with friends. When school finishes around 3:00 PM, I usually head straight home. The afternoon is for homework and sometimes a short break for a snack. Later, I might help with chores or spend some time on a hobby. Dinner with my family is always nice. By 9:00 PM, I'm getting ready for bed, making sure my bag is ready for the next day. This routine helps me stay organized and prepared for school.
